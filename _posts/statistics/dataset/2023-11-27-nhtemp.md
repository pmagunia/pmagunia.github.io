<h2>Average Yearly Temperatures in New Haven</h2>
<p>The mean annual temperature in degrees Fahrenheit in New Haven, Connecticut, from 1912 to 1971.</p>
<h3>Usage</h3>
<pre>nhtemp</pre>
<h3>Format</h3>
<p>A time series of 60 observations.</p>
